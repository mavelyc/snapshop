    SnapShop what you see

    See something you like? SnapShop it! Simply take a picture of the article of clothing you like and in no time you will receive links to stores online where you can buy that article of clothing

    SnapShop by the brand

    Our software is able to detect logos and brand names on clothing and able to link you to the company website with the closest matching article of clothing

    Upload Photo

    SnapShop by style

    Perhaps you are more interested in the style of clothing versus the brand. SnapShop can also search through multiple online stores for you returning the closest looking article of clothing regardless the brand or if a logo is not detected
